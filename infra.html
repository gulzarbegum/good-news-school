INFRASTRUCTURE OF SCHOOL

School Infra: The main structure where the school operates. It may include administrative offices, classrooms, and common areas.






Classrooms: Individual spaces for teaching and learning. These are equipped with desks, chairs, chalkboards or whiteboards, and audio-visual equipment for modern teaching methods.



Library: A dedicated space for students and teachers to access a wide range of books, reference materials, and digital resources.




Computer Labs and Science Labs: : Specialized rooms equipped with computers and internet access for computer-based learning and research.Laboratories for conducting experiments and practical science lessons.




Playgrounds: Outdoor spaces for recreation, including playground equipment.
